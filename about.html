About This Terminal

SYSTEM INFO // TERMINAL ACCESS PROTOCOL

This terminal provides access to field dispatches — short transmissions filed by correspondents operating in remote and restricted sectors. Each dispatch is accompanied by visual documentation captured during the assignment.

Dispatches are filed approximately once per standard cycle. Access levels vary by classification. Some transmissions have been partially redacted for operational security.

The correspondents filing these reports operate independently. The views, observations, and conclusions contained in each dispatch are those of the correspondent alone and do not represent the position of any governing body, military branch, or corporate entity.

Technical

Each dispatch consists of 2-3 paragraphs of field reporting accompanied by commissioned visual documentation. Stories and artwork are original works.
